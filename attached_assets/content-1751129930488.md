### <Manage clients.=

Sync, save, and support all sorts of customer details to streamline bookings and appointments, to build loyalty programs, to sell gift cards, to offer discount codes, and more.

Most popular APIs and tools for customer engagement:

1. [Customers API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/customers-api/what-it-does)
2. [Bookings API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/bookings-api/what-it-is)
3. [Loyalty API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/loyalty-api/overview)
4. [Gift Cards API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/gift-cards/using-gift-cards-api)

### <Analyze data.=

Pull metrics, pay taxes, and keep your books in order with APIs and endpoints that allow users to monitor the health of their businesses.

Most popular APIs and tools for visualizing data:

1. [ListPayments](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/payments-api/retrieve-payments#get-a-list-of-payment-objects)
2. [ListPayouts](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/payouts-api/list-payouts)
3. [SearchOrders](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/orders-api/manage-orders/search-orders)

### <Sell everywhere.=

Whether customers are strolling — or scrolling — through your store, you can build a payment solution to meet them wherever they shop.

Most popular APIs for seamless selling and payment integrations:

1. [Terminal API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/terminal-api/overview)
2. [Reader SDK](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/reader-sdk/what-it-does)
3. [Web Payments SDK](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/web-payments/overview)
4. [Checkout API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/checkout-api)
5. [Subscriptions API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/subscriptions/overview)
6. [Invoices API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/invoices-api/overview)

### <Deliver orders.=

Bring order to your logistical flow with APIs that can manage purchases and deliveries; inventory updates; and your locations, vendors, and merchants.

Most popular APIs for smooth deliveries:

1. [Orders API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/orders-api/what-it-does)
2. [Catalog API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/catalog-api/what-it-does)
3. [Inventory API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/inventory-api/what-it-does)
4. [Locations API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/locations-api)
5. [Vendors API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/vendors-api/manage-vendors-in-apps)
6. [Merchants API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/merchants-api)

### <Move money.=

Manage your banking operations and transfer money directly between accounts.

Most popular APIs for making money move:

1. [Payments API](https://developer.squareup.com/docs/payments-refunds)
